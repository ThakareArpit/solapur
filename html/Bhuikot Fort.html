History

Solapur has a fort known as Bhuikot fort ( bhui means land and kot means fort - as this fort was built on land as against the other forts in Maharashtra built on hills !!). by Adil Shah. The fort later came under Maratha control.


Things to see

1] The grounds, known to host wrestling matches.
2] The Iconic Shiva temple.
3] A small animal park housing several rabbits, ducks, peacocks and other animals.
